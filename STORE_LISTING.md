# Store Listing Templates

Use these for Google Play and App Store Connect.

## Short description (80 chars max - Play Store)

```
Track income and expenses. All data stays on your device. No account needed.
```

## Full description (Play Store / App Store)

```
Spendly helps you track income and expenses simply and privately.

• Dashboard – See total expenses, balance, and income at a glance
• Report – Calendar view and pie charts by category
• Add transactions – Quick entry with categories and dates
• Recurring transactions – Templates with reminders
• Export/Import – Backup and restore your data
• Multiple currencies – IDR, USD, EUR
• No account required – Everything stays on your device
• Open support link – Tip the developer via Saweria

Perfect for personal budgeting. Your data never leaves your phone.
```
